Our Destinations


Chennai

Explore the vibrant capital with its famous Marina Beach, historic Fort St. George, and bustling local markets.

Madurai

Discover the city’s rich history and the magnificent Meenakshi Amman Temple.

Ooty

Escape to the cool hill station with lush greenery, scenic mountain views, and charming tea plantations.

Kanyakumari

Visit the southern tip of India, where the Bay of Bengal, Arabian Sea, and Indian Ocean meet.

Rameswaram

A spiritual hub known for its beautiful temples and clear blue waters, ideal for a mix of spiritual and leisurely exploration.
